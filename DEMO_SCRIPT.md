> "[Screen focus on Deal Analyser page] First, the Deal Analyser. I enter a Leeds terrace: £125,000 purchase, £18,000 refurb, £165,000 GDV, £840 monthly rent.

> Every score is explainable and auditable — that's the AI governance layer baked into every analysis."
